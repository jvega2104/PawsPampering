FAQ's

How often should I be grooming my pet?

Typically dogs with short hair need less maintenance compared to a dog with long hair. Typically your groomer would preference every fout to six weeks! Brushing your pets hair can give you a longer gap between visits.

How long does Dog Grooming take?

We try to make the most of our time and take care of our guests for about 3 to 4 hours based on dog breed.

How much does Service cost?

Our dog grooming services are based on the size of your friend! The price starts at $25.00 and goes up with add-ons such as brushing teeth, nail trims, ear cleaning and so on.

What services do you provide?

Our services include Dog grooming, Flea baths, Nail trims, Tooth brushing, Ear cleaning, Full grooming packages, and other services at request! Let us know what works best with yor pet and we can workout what would be best for your pup.
